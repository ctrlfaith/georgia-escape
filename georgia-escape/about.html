About Georgia Escape

Crafting Unforgettable Journeys in Georgia

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Georgia Escape was born from a passion for sharing Georgia’s hidden gems with the world. Our founders, inspired by the country’s rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality, set out to create curated travel experiences that go beyond the ordinary. From the vibrant streets of Tbilisi to the serene vineyards of Kakheti and the majestic peaks of Kazbegi, we design tours that blend adventure, relaxation, and cultural immersion.

At Georgia Escape, we believe travel is about connection—connecting with people, places, and stories. That’s why every tour is thoughtfully crafted to offer authentic experiences, guided by locals who know Georgia best.

Our Mission

Authentic Experiences
