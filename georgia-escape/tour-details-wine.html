Wine & Wellness

4 Days / 3 Nights · From $590

Overview

Treat yourself to a rejuvenating journey through Georgia’s famous Kakheti wine region. Experience curated tastings, spa indulgence, and tranquil countryside escapes that soothe your body and soul.

  • Boutique vineyard stays with spa facilities
  • Wine tours & sommelier-led tastings
  • Spa sessions and relaxation activities
  • Private transfers and local guide

What's Included

Included
  • Boutique vineyard accommodations (3 nights)
  • Daily breakfast and one dinner
  • Private transfers from/to Tbilisi
  • Wine tours and sommelier-led tastings
  • One spa treatment per guest
Not Included
  • International flights
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips for guide and driver
  • Additional spa treatments or yoga sessions
  • Personal expenses

Itinerary

1
Day 1: Welcome to Kakheti

• Private transfer from Tbilisi to a boutique vineyard resort
• Check-in and unwind with welcome wine tasting
• Evening dinner with vineyard views

2
Day 2: Wine Discovery & Wellness

• Guided wine cellar tour and Qvevri wine session
• Sommelier-led tasting & food pairing
• Afternoon spa treatment or poolside leisure

3
Day 3: Village of Sighnaghi

• Morning drive to Sighnaghi, the city of love
• Explore cobblestone streets, artisan shops
• Optional massage or yoga session in the evening

4
Day 4: Departure

• Leisurely breakfast
• Final stroll or souvenir shopping
• Return transfer to Tbilisi airport or hotel
