Highland Adventure

7 Days / 6 Nights · From $880

Overview

Embark on a thrilling 7-day expedition to Georgia’s spectacular mountain region. Traverse breathtaking landscapes, visit alpine villages, and marvel at the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church under the shadow of Mount Kazbegi.

  • 3- and 4-star mountain lodge accommodations
  • Private 4WD transport through scenic routes
  • English-speaking adventure guide
  • Guided hikes & cultural experiences

What's Included

Included
  • 3- and 4-star mountain lodge accommodations (6 nights)
  • Daily breakfast and welcome dinner
  • Private 4WD airport transfers and tour transport
  • English-speaking adventure guide
  • Guided hikes and cultural activities
Not Included
  • International flights
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips for guide and driver
  • Optional activities (e.g., hot springs, stargazing)
  • Personal expenses

Itinerary

1
Day 1: Arrival in Tbilisi

• Afternoon arrival and airport pickup
• Check-in and trip briefing with your mountain guide
• Welcome dinner with local specialties

2
Day 2: Scenic Drive to Kazbegi via Gudauri

• Stop at Ananuri Fortress
• Lunch with mountain view in Gudauri
• Cross Jvari Pass (2,395m) to Stepantsminda

3
Day 3: Hike to Gergeti Trinity Church

• Morning hike or 4WD ride to the iconic church
• Afternoon leisure or optional hot spring visit

4
Day 4: Dariali Gorge and Gveleti Waterfalls

• Half-day hike through alpine trails and villages
• Picnic lunch near waterfall
• Return to lodge and optional folk music night

5
Day 5: Local Life Experience

• Visit a mountain farm
• Learn traditional bread baking and cheese making
• Evening free or join optional stargazing

6
Day 6: Return to Tbilisi

• Scenic descent via the Military Highway
• Photo stops and lunch en route
• Free evening in Tbilisi for shopping or spa

7
Day 7: Departure

• Hotel breakfast
• Private airport transfer and farewell from guide
